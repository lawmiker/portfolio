ABOUT

Hi, I'm Mike*!

I studied Computer Science with a focus on Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), where I developed a strong interest in creating engaging and meaningful user experiences. My biggest passion is game development, and one of my long-term goals is to create my own game.

I'm particularly interested in exploring how video games can interact with biomechanics and physiological signals, such as heart rate, facial expressions, and breathing patterns, to create more immersive and versatile gameplay while also providing a background to improve the player's psychological state.

Aside from that I'm working with Java, TypeScript, HTML, CSS, and Angular for frontend web development.

Outside of programming, I enjoy digital art and have recently gotten into learning 3D character modeling.

This website is not much of a show for now - it's rather a place for me to track and share progress, experiment with new ideas, and document projects as I continue to learn and grow.
